Deep Learning is a region of research in machine perception. Since classifying images, sound, text and time series data by Deep Learning breaks record after record, it has been attracting huge attention in recent years.

In this talk, OE_uia, designer of DeepLearning4s andd ND4S and a contributor of 4j, explains the fundamental of Deep Learning as well as Scala DSL for Deep Learning4j and ND4S, N dimensional array calculation library.
