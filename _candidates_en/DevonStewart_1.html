Follow decisions, techniques, and goals, as we try to refactor from the Typesafe Stack to a more purely functional codebase. Topics covered will include Encoding safety into the typesystem, refactoring out common typeclasses, and effective modeling of state using sealed traits.

An understanding of purely functional practices (Haskell, scalaz) is not required, concepts will be explained with examples.
