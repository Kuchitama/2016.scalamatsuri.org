Lodeo, an ad network for videos, was developed by a group of Scala beginners: 6 total with zero experience, and with 4 with less than a half year of Scala experience. In this session, I would like to share our experience of developing Lodeo in Scala by comparing early phase of the project to middle phase of it with 2 beginners, and introduce good/bad practices of the development.

The following is the outline:

  • Converting from Java to Scala
  • Helpful tips on writing comments for the PR reviews
  • The scalaz dispute at Lodeo
