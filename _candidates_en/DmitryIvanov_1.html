In this talk we are going to show how you can benefit from our custom CRDT-based model, called OUR-Set, to distribute data replicas on client devices.

CRDTs resolve data conflicts locally, and this simplifies the synchronization between replicas and makes it robust in unreliable networks.

We show a basic implementation of OUR-Set (Observed, Updated, Removed) in Scala.
