The vast majority of Scala programmers spend all day staring at a black-and-white text output or colored-rectangles in a web browser.

As it turns out, computers can do much more than generate black-and-white text or colored rectangles. Furthermore, it isn't even very hard. This talk will explore the ways you can turn Scala into literal art-forms, creating images and scenes that are tedious or impossible to do by hand in Photoshop.
