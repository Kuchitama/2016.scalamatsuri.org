Even if people don't know logic programming languages as represented by Prolog, many people should know about query languages such as SQL. Logic programming is able to express more general relationships in declartive manner.

In this session, I will present a logic programming library implemented by Scala and its application, and introduce the essence of it, the Logic monad.
