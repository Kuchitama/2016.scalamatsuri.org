The annual Hatena internship has added a Scala curriculum from this year.

In this talk, I will go over my summer with interns, including authoring an intern textbook from scratch, prior challenges, on-boarding, and product development in the Mackerel team.

I hope to share some useful hints from our intership process on the engineer education, which is becoming a hot topic as Scala spreads more widely.
